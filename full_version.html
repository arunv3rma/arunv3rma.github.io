Arun Verma

I am a Research Fellow (Postdoctoral) in Department of Computer Science at National University of Singapore from May 2021, advised by Assoc. Prof. Bryan Kian Hsiang Low.

I obtained my Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay), supervised by Prof. Manjesh K. Hanawal and Prof. N. Hemachandra. During my Ph.D., I spent six months with Dr. Odalric-Ambrym Maillard at SequeL Team (now SCOOL) of INRIA Lille, France as Raman-Charpak Fellow and three months at Conduent Labs India. Before my current position, I was a Research Associate at IIT Bombay.

My research interests broadly lie in designing and analyzing algorithms for data-driven sequential decision-making problems, which can learn efficiently and effectively by exploiting the underlying problem structure. More recently, I am working on sequential decision-making problems under uncertainty, including multi-armed bandit, Bayesian optimization, multi-agent learning, and reinforcement learning.
