Arun Verma

I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate in Manus, and Machina (M3S) team at the Singapore-MIT Alliance for Research and Technology (SMART), where I am working with Prof. Daniela Rus and Prof. Bryan Kian Hsiang Low.

Prior to this, I was a Research Fellow in Department of Computer Science at National University of Singapore, where I was working with Prof. Bryan Kian Hsiang Low. I obtained my Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) degree from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay), where Prof. Manjesh K. Hanawal and Prof. N. Hemachandra supervised me. My doctoral thesis received two awards: Naik and Rastogi Excellence in Ph. D. Thesis Award and COMSNETS Best Ph.D. Thesis Award.

My research focus on designing and analyzing efficient and practical algorithms for sequential decision-making, particularly problems in areas such as active learning, Bayesian optimization, and reinforcement learning. I am especially interested in using these algorithms to develop efficient and effective LLMs and adaptive AI systems.
