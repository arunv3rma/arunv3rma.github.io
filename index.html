Arun Verma

I am a Research Fellow in Department of Computer Science at NUS from May 2021, advised by Prof. Bryan Kian Hsiang Low.

I obtained my Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay), supervised by Prof. Manjesh K. Hanawal and Prof. N. Hemachandra. My Ph.D. thesis received Naik and Rastogi Excellence in Ph. D. Thesis Award and COMSNETS Best Ph.D. Thesis Award. During my Ph.D., I spent six months with Dr. Odalric-Ambrym Maillard at SequeL Team (now SCOOL) of INRIA Lille, France as Raman-Charpak Fellow and three months at Conduent Labs India. Before my current position, I was a Research Associate at IIT Bombay.

My research interest lies in designing and analyzing the practical data-driven algorithms for sequential decision-making under uncertainty, which can learn efficiently and effectively by exploiting the underlying problem structure. My research has recently focused on sequential decision-making problems in multi-armed bandits, Bayesian optimization, and reinforcement learning.
